About this Event

The Coding Contest challenges participants to solve algorithmic problems under timed conditions. Problems range from beginner to advanced, testing logic, data structures, and optimization techniques. Teams or individuals are welcome. Results will be announced at the closing ceremony of Gateways 2026.

Rules and Guidelines
  • Participants must carry their university ID card at the time of the event.
  • Only individual participation is allowed for this event.
  • Use of internet, mobile phones, or external resources is prohibited during the contest.
  • Allowed languages: C, C++, Java, Python.
  • Plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Decisions of the organizing committee are final.
